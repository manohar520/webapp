Classic Iced Coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or cream
  • Sugar or sweetener (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the coffee over the ice.
  4. Add milk or cream to taste.
  5. Add sugar or sweetener if desired.
